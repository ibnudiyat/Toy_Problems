Net Salary Calculator

Calculation Details

Calculation Logic: The net salary is calculated by subtracting PAYE, NHIF, and NSSF deductions from the gross salary.

Formulas:

  • PAYE = Total Income * Applicable KRA Rate
  • NHIF Deductions = Total Income * NHIF Rate
  • NSSF Deductions = Total Income * NSSF Rate
  • Gross Salary = Total Income - PAYE - NHIF Deductions - NSSF Deductions
  • Net Salary = Gross Salary - PAYE - NHIF Deductions - NSSF Deductions
